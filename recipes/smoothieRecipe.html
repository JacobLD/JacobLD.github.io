    Ingredients

    -

  • Frozen Banana and Strawberry mix
  • Greek Yogurt - Vanilla
  • Milk
  • Optional - Vanilla Protein Powder

-

Directions
  • Throw ingredients into a blender and go to town. The only important measurement is to not add too much fruit in the beginning. Everything else can be modified on the fly.
  • A rough estimate would be half a blenders worth of fruit. Two decent spoonfuls of yogurt. One scoop of powder if you choose to use it. Milk up to 80% of the way to the top of the fruit. Add more milk if it doens't wish to blend.
